Hot Dog Mummies

Description

The recipe for "Hot Dog Mummies" from Cravings by Chrissy Teigen is a playful and creative take on classic hot dogs that's perfect for Halloween or any occasion that calls for some spooky, fun food. This recipe transforms ordinary hot dogs into adorable, edible mummies that will delight both kids and adults. By wrapping each hot dog in strips of crescent roll dough, the result is a golden, flaky "bandage" that bakes up into a crispy shell. The use of black mustard seeds as "eyes" adds a whimsical touch to these mummy dogs.

Not only are Hot Dog Mummies a crowd-pleaser, but they are also incredibly easy to prepare, making them an ideal choice for party appetizers or a fun family dinner. The combination of savory hot dogs with the buttery, slightly sweet crescent roll dough creates a satisfying contrast in textures and flavors. With just a touch of creativity and a few simple ingredients, these Hot Dog Mummies bring a touch of spooky whimsy to your table, making them a hit at Halloween gatherings or anytime you want to add some fun to your meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 8 oz. can crescent dough
  • 12 hot dogs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Yellow mustard, for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment paper. Separate the crescent dough into 4 rectangles, then use a sharp knife to cut each rectangle into ¼-inch strips the long way. You should have 12 rectangles.
  2. Use 3 or 4 strips of dough to wrap each hot dog, crisscrossing sometimes and leaving space towards the top for “eyes,” so each dog looks like a bundled mummy.
  3. Arrange on the baking sheet, brush with butter, and bake until the dough is puffed, flaky, and lightly golden, 15–17 minutes.
  4. Use a toothpick or wooden skewer to create “eyes” on each mummy with mustard. Serve with more dipping sauces, such as ketchup.
